THE DEBUGGER

Student would be taught 3 electronic circuits/ configurations
used most frequently in the different gadgets.
After teaching them the circuits, an erratic arrangement of the components would be given to
them. Within a specific time limit, they have to rectify the error in the
circuit and make it functioning.

Rules :


Students would not require to bring anything or have any
prerequisite knowledge in this field. During the first one hour,
presentations and demonstrations would be shown to the participants.
Then in the next one hour, the debugging competition would be held.

Judging Criteria :


To qualify the challenge, the participants must make
the circuit functioning. Three teams who qualify the challenge to the earliest would be declared as winners.
